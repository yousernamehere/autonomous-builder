# Execution Plays (v0)

## Play 1 — Landing + Lead Capture (MVP)
- Goal: publish a single-page offer with an email capture CTA.
- Metric: visitor → email conversion.

## Play 2 — Outbound Sprint (20 messages/day)
- Goal: start conversations with a tight ICP.
- Metric: reply rate, meetings booked.

## Play 3 — Content Flywheel (weekly)
- Goal: consistent publishing tied to an offer.
- Metric: subscribers + clicks.
